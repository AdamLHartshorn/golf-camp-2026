# Draft Notes

Golf Camp official morning rounds are played Wednesday through Saturday.

Drafts happen the night before each official round:
- Tuesday night drafts Wednesday teams
- Wednesday night drafts Thursday teams
- Thursday night drafts Friday teams
- Friday night drafts Saturday teams

The draft is commissioner-driven. Nick runs the draft.

Current desired future workflow:
- Nick controls the draft from his phone.
- A laptop is connected to the TV/projector.
- The TV displays the live draft board.
- Captains are seated with the group, not operating devices.
- Nick asks who the captain is drafting.
- Nick enters the selected player.
- TV updates automatically.

The TV draft board only needs to clearly show:
- who is currently drafting
- the available player pool
- teams as they are being drafted

The draft should preserve the existing room energy. The app should support the ritual, not overproduce or sterilize it.

Player ranks:
- Public ranks are A, B, C, D.
- Internal ordering like A1, A2, B1, B2 is for commissioner organization only.
- Internal ordering may be used for sorting the available pool.
- Internal ordering should not be displayed publicly.
